What to do now?

Welcome to your Public Venue!

Now that your venue is public, you have access to more features!


The first thing you should do is use the "Price this Venue" button on the right to set a price for the sponsorship. This is the price for 1 year of sponsorship on your venue.


Next, you should set up some SPUDS. There are two ways to do this: Filter Posts from Instagram, and Posting SPUDS on Twitter.

Filtering Posts from Instagram can be done at this link: Filter Instagram Posts. This link shows you a list of posts Geo-Tagged within the bounds of your venue, and allows you to approve the ones that are related to the venue/sport.

Posting SPUDS on Twitter requires a Fan account on Spudder. Once you Follow this venue with a Spudder Fan account, you can create Tweets with "@postspud" and "#<yourtagforthisvenue>" and an image, and they will get automatically linked to this Venue!


Remember, the more content your Venue has, the more likely you are to get a Sponsorship, so always keep your info updated, and keep making SPUDS!
