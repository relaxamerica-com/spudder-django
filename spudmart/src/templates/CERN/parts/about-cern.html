What is CERN?

CERN stands for Campus Entrepreneur Recruiting Network. CERN is made up of students at accredited colleges and universities in the United States.

What do these students do?

Members of CERN participate in all sorts of projects! Our five main projects are Marketing, Social Media PR, Content Management (Blogging), Design, and Quality Assurance (Bug Testing). Scroll down to learn a little more about the projects.

How do I get started on a project?

All you have to do to begin is click on the page for that project. You can either use the toolbar to the left or scroll down and click on the button that says "Start this project!" below.
