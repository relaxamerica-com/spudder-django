What is "CERN"?

CERN stands for Campus Entrepreneur Recruiting Network. CERN Teams are made up of people associated with colleges and Universities in the United States.

What do these students do?

As a member of a CERN Team, you can learn critical skills and showcase your talents as an entrepreneur on various projects.

Currently, we have projects for Marketing and Social Media PR, with more coming soon!

What do I get out of joining a CERN Team?

In addition to enhancing your resume with third party validated real world accomplishments, you’ll actually get paid for the work you do!

Different projects earn different kinds of rewards – sign up to learn more!
