What to do now?

Welcome to your new Venue!

Venues are the core commodity in the CERN system. They are yours to claim, promote and monetize!

As the venue controller, its your responsibility to make this venue an attractive Sponsorship target.

If your venue info is up-to-date, and has a decent amount of content associated with it, Sponsors will Pay to sponsor it!


Start by entering 'edit mode' and giving this venue a name and a common name, and uploading an image or icon for this venue.

Then cycle through the various venue properties adding and maintaining content.

Remember the the more content you add, the better chance of winning a Sponsorship!


Coming Soon: Soon you will be able to curate auto generated SPUDS providing a rich stream of content associated with your venue!
