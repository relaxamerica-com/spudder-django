What is "CERN"?

CERN stands for Campus Entrepreneur Recruiting Network. CERN Teams are made up of students currently attending accredited colleges and universities in the United States.

What do these students do?

As a member of a CERN Team, you can participate in a number of projects related to entrepreneurial fields. Currently, we have projects for Marketing and Social Media PR, with more coming soon!
