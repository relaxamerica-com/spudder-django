Why Create Teams?

Teams need to be created in order to Tag Spuds to a team, So if you can not find the Team you are looking for, you are going to need to create. The information For creating the Team is very simple and straightforward.


NOTE: Creating a team doesn't give you the right to fundraise on that teams behalf. Only teams that are associated with a CLUB - (the legal entity approved to fundraise can fundraise on behalf of a team) The team you're creating is a Social Page that holds the SPUDs and basic information about the TEAM
