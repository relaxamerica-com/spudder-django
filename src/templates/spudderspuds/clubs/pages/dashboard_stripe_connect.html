Want to know more about Stripe?

What is Stripe?
Stripe is an online payment service that make is super easy for organizations like {{ club_entity.name }} to collect donations and payments online.
What do I need to connect
with Stripe
When you click the blue button above, you'll be taken to a Stripe registration page where you'll be asked to enter the details of {{ club_entity.name }}. Most importantly, you'll need to have the following to hand:
  • The full legal name of {{ club_entity.name }} (As registered with the IRS)
  • Your organizations EIN (Tax ID) (As given to you by the IRS)
  • The routing number and account number of your organizations bank account (So you can start fundraising!)
What if I don't have
these right now?
It's not a problem. You can always get back to this screen by visiting www.spudder.com and logging in with the email address and password you used when registering.
