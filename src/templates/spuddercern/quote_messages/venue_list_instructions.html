What should I do with my Venues?

Venues are locations where sports games get played. They have are tied to a physical location, so that we can find posts on other social media sites that you can turn into SPUDS.

The first thing you should do is give your venue a name and a custom @tag. The @tag is how people will be able to reference your venue in social media posts that you can turn into SPUDS.

While you're on the venue page, you should also fill out as much additional information as you can. The more you have filled out, the more likely it is that a local business will pay for a sponsorship. When your venue gets sponsored, you earn a percentage of the sponsorship!

SPUDS: On the far right you will see a button with a small camera (Instagram) icon. When you click on it, you will be directed to a page where you can accept or reject SPUDS for the given venue.
